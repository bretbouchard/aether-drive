## Risk Assessment

### High-Priority Risks

**1. Technical Issues (CRITICAL)**
- **Risk**: Critical bugs discovered post-launch
- **Probability**: LOW (extensive beta testing planned)
- **Impact**: HIGH (could damage reputation)
- **Mitigation**: 50 beta testers, rapid response team, hotfix plan
- **Contingency**: Hotfix release within 48 hours

**2. Distribution Delays (HIGH)**
- **Risk**: Retail partner approval delays
- **Probability**: MEDIUM (common in industry)
- **Impact**: MEDIUM (reduced launch reach)
- **Mitigation**: Submit to partners 4 weeks early, website as backup
- **Contingency**: Focus on direct sales if partners delayed

**3. Marketing Underperformance (MEDIUM)**
- **Risk**: Low conversion rates or weak sales
- **Probability**: LOW (strong product and positioning)
- **Impact**: HIGH (miss revenue targets)
- **Mitigation**: A/B testing, multiple channels, promotional pricing
- **Contingency**: Increase ad spend, deeper discounts, bundle deals

**4. Negative Reviews (MEDIUM)**
- **Risk**: Poor early reviews impact sales
- **Probability**: LOW (product quality is excellent)
- **Impact**: HIGH (reputation damage)
- **Mitigation**: Extensive beta testing, preview copies to friendly reviewers
- **Contingency**: Rapid issue resolution, public response to concerns

### Medium-Priority Risks

**5. Competitor Launch (LOW)**
- **Risk**: Major competitor releases similar product
- **Probability**: LOW (cannot predict competitor actions)
- **Impact**: MEDIUM (increased competition)
- **Mitigation**: Emphasize unique features, competitive pricing
- **Contingency**: Differentiation messaging, bundle offers

**6. Support Overload (LOW)**
- **Risk**: High support volume overwhelming team
- **Probability**: MEDIUM (common at launch)
- **Impact**: MEDIUM (response time delays)
- **Mitigation**: Comprehensive documentation, FAQ, community support
- **Contingency**: Hire additional support staff, extended response times
